// Section B · 360ops Spartan (local deployment)

B.1 What Spartan is

360ops Spartan runs entirely on physical hardware you own or control ("Box"). Scrubbing, redaction, scrub vault, agent memory, and (where your hardware allows) local AI inference all happen on your hardware.

B.2 Hardware binding

The Spartan license is bound to specific hardware units registered at activation. Each Box is identified by a hardware fingerprint (e.g. MAC + RAM signature). You may not operate Spartan on hardware exceeding your tier limit without prior written authorization.

B.3 Subscription pricing

Spartan tier pricing (boxes, users, included scrub volume) is published at 360ops.ai and is subject to change with 30 days' notice.

B.4 License key

Your license key authenticates your Box with 360ops license servers. Do not share, publish, or transfer the key. If compromised, contact support@360ops.ai immediately.

B.5 Data sovereignty — Spartan

All your customer data — including data processed, stored, or generated by Spartan — resides exclusively on your hardware. 360ops has zero access to your customer data. The only information transmitted to 360ops servers is:

  • Your license key
  • A hardware fingerprint
  • Software version
  • Aggregate counts (active users, memory utilization, agent names)

No business data, AI conversations, financial records, customer or employee information, files, or content of any kind is transmitted.

B.6 Hardware transfer

If you replace or upgrade hardware, notify support@360ops.ai. We reissue your license binding within 2 business days.

B.7 Offline grace

If Spartan can't reach 360ops license servers, it continues operating in offline grace mode. Spartan will not disable itself due to server unreachability.

// Section C · 360ops Desktop & Portal

C.1 360ops Desktop (Windows binary)

The 360ops Desktop is a Windows binary that runs locally on your machine. It performs scrubbing, holds the scrub vault, routes scrubbed AI traffic to the providers you authorize, and substitutes original values back into replies. It does not transmit your prompts or replies to 360ops servers.

C.2 360ops Portal (cloud account UI)

The Portal is a cloud-hosted web application for account management, billing, integrations, team administration, and module activation. The Portal does not handle AI prompts or replies. The privacy filter does not run in the Portal.

C.3 Cloud infrastructure

The Portal is hosted on US-based infrastructure under SOC 2-aligned vendors. Customer account data is encrypted in transit and at rest. 360ops does not sell your data and does not use your data to train AI models. The current sub-processor list is published and updated at 360ops.ai/security.

C.4 Account-based subscription

Portal access is account-based — there is no hardware binding. Your subscription grants access through any supported web browser. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

C.5 Uptime & maintenance

We use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain Portal availability. Planned maintenance is announced when feasible. The Portal is provided on a best-efforts basis without uptime guarantees during the v0.x.x prelaunch period.
