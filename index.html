// What ops means

Three pillars. One promise.

Use AI without giving it your data. The same three pillars hold whether you run a healthcare practice or a personal life.

O Operate

You operate the AI. Not the other way around. You decide what gets sent where, what stays local, and what gets retained.

For business

Your team directs every AI workflow. Route by sensitivity, by model, by cost. No vendor decides what your data is used for.

P Protection

Your data is structurally protected before it leaves your control. Field-level scrubbing, redaction, and classification — built in.

For business

PHI, PII, financial fields, privileged matter — scrubbed before any cloud call. HIPAA, CMMC, SOC 2 ready.

S Sovereignty

You decide where your data lives. On your hardware, in your region, under your rules. No exceptions.

For business

On-prem Spartan, sovereign cloud, regional residency. True air-gap option for regulated work.

// What 360ops is

  • An AI privacy interface. A filter between you and the cloud providers you choose.
  • A local scrubber. PHI, PII, financial fields stripped before any cloud call.
  • A token-restoration layer. Cloud sees placeholders; we restore real values on your device.
  • Compliance foundation. HIPAA, GDPR, CCPA, CMMC, SOC 2 workflows.

// What 360ops is not

  • Not an AI model. We don't build foundation models.
  • Not a training pipeline. Your content never trains anything.
  • Not a data broker. We don't see your prompts or replies.
  • Not a black box. The scrub vault stays on your device.
