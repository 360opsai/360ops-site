360ops // privacy policy

Your data is yours.

Effective May 3, 2026 · Version 1.0 · 360opsAI LLC, a Florida limited liability company

360opsAI LLC, a Florida limited liability company doing business as 360ops ("360ops," "we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect across our two products, how we use it, and your rights.

The short version: 360ops Spartan keeps your data entirely on your hardware — we never see it. 360ops Portal processes data on cloud infrastructure with full transparency about our sub-processors.

// 01 · Our two products and their privacy models

1.1 360ops Spartan — Local Deployment

360ops Spartan runs entirely on hardware you own and control. All AI processing, data storage, agent memory, conversation history, and business intelligence happens locally on your box. 360ops servers are contacted only for license validation, hourly heartbeat pings, and software updates you initiate.

360ops has zero access to your Spartan Customer Data.

1.2 360ops Portal — Cloud SaaS

360ops Portal is a cloud-hosted product. Customer data is processed on US-based cloud infrastructure. 360ops and its sub-processors access data as necessary to provide the service. See Section 4 for full details.

// 02 · Information we collect

2.1 Account and Billing Information (Both Products)

When you purchase a subscription:

  • Business name and contact information
  • Billing address and payment information (processed by Stripe)
  • Email address of the account administrator
  • Subscription tier and payment history

2.2 Spartan License Validation Data

Every 24 hours, Spartan automatically transmits to 360ops license servers:

  • Your license key
  • A hardware fingerprint (MAC address + RAM configuration)
  • Software version number
  • Aggregate usage counts: number of active users, memory usage percentage, names of active AI agents

This data does NOT include: conversation content, business data, customer information, financial records, employee information, AI outputs, or any other business content.

2.3 Spartan Heartbeat Data

Every hour, Spartan sends a minimal ping containing: license key, box identifier (hostname), and current memory usage. This confirms your system is operational.

2.4 Portal Customer Data

When you use the Portal, we process:

  • Data you enter, upload, or create within the Portal
  • AI conversation history and agent outputs
  • Business data you connect through integrations
  • User account information and activity logs

2.5 Support Communications

If you contact us for support, we collect information you provide including error logs or diagnostic information you choose to share.

2.6 Website and Console Analytics

Standard web analytics including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs when you access our website or management console.

// 03 · Information we do NOT collect (Spartan)

When using 360ops Spartan, 360ops does NOT collect, access, or store:

  • Business conversations with AI agents
  • Documents, files, or data you upload to Spartan
  • Customer, employee, or vendor information stored in Spartan
  • Financial records or transactions processed by Spartan
  • AI agent memories, learned behaviors, or knowledge
  • Emails, calendar data, or other integrated service content
  • Any personally identifiable information of your employees or customers

// 04 · Portal data processing

4.1 How Portal Data Is Processed

Portal Customer Data is processed on US-based infrastructure. When you submit an AI request through the Portal:

  • The request is processed by our AI inference providers (Anthropic, Groq)
  • The request and response are stored in our Supabase database
  • The Portal is hosted on Railway infrastructure

4.2 360ops Access to Portal Data

360ops team members may access Portal Customer Data for:

  • Providing customer support when requested
  • Investigating security incidents
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Monitoring for abuse or violations of these Terms

360ops does not access Portal data for any other purpose without your consent.

4.3 Portal Sub-Processors

// 05 · How we use information (both products)

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Validate and enforce software licenses;
  2. Process payments and manage subscriptions;
  3. Provide customer support;
  4. Monitor system health and alert you to issues;
  5. Improve the Software based on aggregate, non-identifiable patterns;
  6. Comply with legal obligations;
  7. Detect and prevent fraud or abuse.

We do NOT use your information to:

  • Train AI models
  • Sell to third parties
  • Serve advertising
  • Profile your business practices

// 06 · Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share limited information only in these circumstances:

6.1 Sub-Processors

We share Portal data with the sub-processors listed in Section 4.3 for the purposes described.

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of 360ops, our customers, or others.

6.3 Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, customer information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

// 07 · Data security

7.1 Our Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews.

7.2 Spartan Security

Spartan data never leaves your hardware. You are responsible for the physical and network security of your Spartan installation.

7.3 Portal Security

Portal data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement access controls to limit who can access Customer Data within our organization.

// 08 · Data retention

8.1 Account Data

We retain account and billing data for the duration of your subscription plus 7 years for compliance purposes.

8.2 Portal Customer Data

Portal Customer Data is retained for the duration of your subscription. Upon termination, Customer Data is deleted within 90 days unless retention is required by law.

8.3 Spartan Customer Data

Spartan data is stored exclusively on your hardware. 360ops has no role in its retention or deletion.

8.4 Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your data by contacting privacy@360ops.ai. We will honor deletion requests within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

// 09 · Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information:

9.1 Access, Correction, and Deletion

You may request a copy of, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting privacy@360ops.ai.

9.2 California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have rights including the right to know, right to delete, and right to opt out of sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. Contact privacy@360ops.ai to exercise your rights.

9.3 European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR. Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (license enforcement) and contract performance. Contact privacy@360ops.ai to exercise your rights or lodge a complaint.

We will respond to privacy rights requests within 30 days.

// 10 · Cookies and tracking

Our management console uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

// 11 · Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or through a notice in the Software at least 30 days before the changes take effect.
