Privacy Policy

Your Data. Your Rights.

We're committed to transparency about what data we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have. GDPR-compliant. Owner-level exports available on demand.

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

📋 What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the 360ops AI Business Operating System. This includes:

  • Account data: Name, email address, role, and organization details provided during registration.
  • Conversation data: Messages exchanged with AI agents within your organization's workspace. Stored per-organization in isolated database instances.
  • Agent configuration: Agent names, roles, departments, and behavioral settings (not including internal AI prompt engineering).
  • Task & workflow data: Tasks created, assigned, and completed through the platform.
  • Knowledge base content: Articles, documents, and reference material added to your org's knowledge base.
  • Usage metadata: Token consumption, request counts, response times, and billing information.
  • Audit logs: Anonymized records of AI queries (hash only — raw query text is never stored), gatekeeper decisions, and data export events.
  • Integration credentials: Encrypted credentials for third-party connectors (QuickBooks, Atera, Slack, etc.) stored in a secure vault.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties.

⚙️ How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to deliver and improve the 360ops platform:

  • AI agent responses: Conversation history and knowledge base content are used to generate contextually relevant AI responses within your organization.
  • Compliance enforcement: The 12-stage Gatekeeper pipeline analyzes query metadata (not content) to enforce RBAC, PII detection, and compliance frameworks.
  • Platform operations: Usage data is used for billing, capacity planning, and service reliability.
  • Security auditing: Audit logs help detect unauthorized access, data anomalies, and compliance violations.
  • Product improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns may inform platform improvements. No individual data is used for model training without explicit consent.

AI models process your data to generate responses. We use leading AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) under strict data processing agreements that prohibit training on your data.

🗄️ Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your organization account is active, plus a brief post-termination window for legal and support purposes:

  • Conversation history: Retained for the life of your account. Exportable at any time.
  • Audit logs: Retained for 12 months by default. Enterprise plans may configure longer retention.
  • Agent tasks & knowledge: Retained indefinitely while your account is active.
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.
  • Post-cancellation: Upon account cancellation, org data is retained for 30 days to allow data export, then permanently deleted within 90 days.

Each organization has a dedicated, isolated database instance. Data from one organization is never accessible to another.

🔒 Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

As a data subject and organization owner, you have the following rights:

⬇️
Right to Export
Download all your organization's data as a structured JSON file directly from Settings → Data & Privacy in the portal. Available to org owners only.
✏️
Right to Correction
Update your account information, agent configurations, and organizational data at any time through the portal. Contact support for corrections we control.
🗑️
Right to Deletion
Request permanent deletion of your organization and all associated data. Email support@360ops.ai with subject "Account Deletion Request". Processed within 30 days.
🚫
Right to Restrict Processing
You can disable AI features, restrict connector access, and configure the Gatekeeper to limit data processing through the portal settings.
📋
Right to Know
You can request a complete account of what personal data we hold about you and how it is used. Contact privacy@360ops.ai.
📤
Right to Portability
All exported data is provided in standard JSON and Markdown formats, ensuring you can migrate to any other platform without lock-in.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

360ops uses minimal, necessary cookies:

  • Authentication cookies: Session tokens required to keep you logged in. Expire after 24 hours of inactivity.
  • Preference cookies: Store UI preferences like theme and sidebar state. Not used for tracking.

We do not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics that share data with ad networks, or fingerprinting technologies. Our analytics (if any) are first-party and anonymized.

🛡️ Security Measures

We apply enterprise-grade security to protect your data:

  • Dedicated database per organization — no shared tenancy
  • All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Credentials stored in a secure vault, never in plaintext
  • 12-stage AI Gatekeeper for PII detection and compliance enforcement
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database layer
  • Audit logging of all sensitive operations including data exports

For a full security overview, see our Security & Compliance page.
