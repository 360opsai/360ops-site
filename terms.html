// 01 · What 360ops is

360ops is an AI interface and privacy filter. It sits between you and the cloud AI providers you choose. We do not run AI models. We don't train AI models. We make sure the sensitive parts of your work never reach the cloud unredacted.

1.1 360ops Desktop & Spartan — local

The 360ops Desktop client and the 360ops Spartan deployment run on hardware you own or control. Scrubbing, redaction, the scrub vault, and (when your hardware allows) local model inference all happen on your device. 360ops servers are contacted only for license validation, software updates, and routing scrubbed traffic to the AI providers you've enabled.

1.2 360ops Portal — cloud-hosted account UI

The Portal is a cloud-hosted web app where you manage your account, billing, integrations, and team. The Portal does not handle AI prompts or replies. The privacy filter runs on your device, not in the Portal.
